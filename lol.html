Gameplay

League of Legends gameplay consists of two teams of champions battling it out on the Rift with the goal of destroying the enemy Nexus.League of Legends is a team-based strategy game where two teams of five players compete to destroy the opposing team's Nexus, a structure located within their base. Each player controls a unique character, known as a "champion," with its own set of abilities and strengths. The game is played on a map called Summoner's Rift, which features three main lanes and a jungle area between them. Players must work together to outmaneuver and outsmart their opponents, earning gold and experience by defeating enemy minions, champions, and neutral monsters.
